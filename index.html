Our Founder

Meet Madam Zulfat Mukarubega

Madam Zulfat Mukarubega is a visionary leader, genocide survivor, and a trailblazer for social change. With unwavering determination and compassion, she has dedicated her life to uplifting marginalized communities, empowering youth, women, and people with disabilities.

Her early experiences in the aftermath of the 1994 Genocide instilled in her a profound understanding of the hardships faced by the vulnerable. This painful past fueled her passion for education and opportunity—believing that every individual holds untapped potential waiting to be realized.

Through her hands-on approach, she has been instrumental in rebuilding communities, establishing local initiatives, and mentoring emerging leaders. Her commitment is reflected in every project, as she works tirelessly to create pathways for sustainable development and hope.

Vision & Mission

Mission: To break the cycle of poverty by empowering disadvantaged youth, women, and people with disabilities through comprehensive programs in education, vocational training, and entrepreneurship. Her mission is rooted in the belief that sustainable change starts by investing in people.

Vision: A Rwanda where every individual is provided with the tools, support, and opportunities to achieve self-reliance and financial independence, fostering a community of dignity, inclusiveness, and lasting hope.

This dual approach ensures that our initiatives are not only addressing immediate needs but also paving the way for long-term, transformative change in communities across Rwanda.

Inspiration & Journey

Madam Zulfat’s journey is a testament to the strength of the human spirit. Rising from the depths of tragedy, she transformed her pain into a powerful drive for community development and social justice. Her path is illuminated by countless stories of resilience and recovery from those she has helped.

Traveling extensively across Rwanda, she listens to community voices and integrates their needs into innovative programs. Her personal experiences have guided her to develop strategies that not only address immediate challenges but also lay the groundwork for long-term transformation.

Her story continues to inspire new generations of leaders who see in her a role model for courage and perseverance.

Celebrating Achievements

Under Madam Zulfat's dynamic leadership, the foundation has reached remarkable milestones. Over 387 students have been sponsored for education, opening doors to brighter futures, while 145 individuals have secured international job placements in competitive markets like the UAE and Qatar.

The foundation has also successfully generated hundreds of local jobs through tailored vocational training programs, each achievement reinforcing her vision of a more empowered and self-sustaining community.

  • Establishment of community learning centers across several districts.
  • Launch of vocational hubs that provide market-ready skills.
  • International collaborations that create global job opportunities.
  • Ongoing mentorship initiatives that nurture the next generation of leaders.

Words from the Founder

"Every act of kindness creates a ripple effect. I believe that by empowering one person, we uplift an entire community. Our work is not just about providing opportunities, but also about nurturing hope and resilience in every heart we touch."

Her heartfelt words continue to inspire not only those within the foundation but also countless individuals and communities far beyond Rwanda's borders. They remind us that every contribution, however small, has the power to drive monumental change.

Who We Are

Our Mission

To break the cycle of poverty by empowering disadvantaged communities through comprehensive education, vocational training, and entrepreneurship. We focus on building the skills and confidence needed for sustainable personal and community growth.

Our programs are designed to provide access to modern educational resources, state-of-the-art vocational training facilities, and innovative business incubation centers. By tailoring our approach to the unique needs of each community, we ensure that every individual receives the support necessary to succeed.

  • Education: Scholarships, literacy programs, and digital skills workshops.
  • Vocational Training: Hands-on skill development in trades and technology.
  • Entrepreneurship: Business start-up mentoring and microfinance opportunities.

Our mission is not just about providing resources—it’s about inspiring hope, building self-esteem, and igniting a passion for lifelong learning.

Our Vision

We envision a Rwanda where every individual is given the opportunity to achieve self-reliance and financial independence. Our goal is a future where education and innovation create a ripple effect that transforms entire communities.

We see a society where barriers to success are eliminated and where collaboration and community spirit drive development. This vision is supported by our commitment to inclusivity and continuous improvement in all our initiatives.

Through strategic investments in people and technology, we aim to cultivate an environment where creativity, critical thinking, and resilience are at the core of progress.

Our Values

Our work is grounded in the principles that shape everything we do. We are committed to:

  • Integrity: Upholding the highest standards of transparency and accountability in all our actions.
  • Compassion: Demonstrating empathy and understanding towards the challenges faced by the communities we serve.
  • Resilience: Fostering a spirit of perseverance and adaptability in the face of adversity.
  • Empowerment: Enabling individuals to take control of their future through skill development and self-confidence.

These values are not just words on paper—they guide our day-to-day operations and our long-term strategies, ensuring that every project we undertake reflects our commitment to social justice and community welfare.

Our History

Born out of the devastation of the 1994 Genocide, Zulfat Foundation was founded in 2008 with a mission to restore dignity and opportunity to those who had been forgotten. Our humble beginnings as a grassroots initiative have evolved into a respected organization that serves hundreds of communities across Rwanda.

Over the years, we have celebrated numerous milestones:

  • Establishment of our first community learning center in 2009.
  • Launch of vocational training programs in 2011, which have since trained over 500 individuals.
  • Expansion of our scholarship fund in 2013, enabling access to higher education for hundreds of students.
  • Partnerships with international organizations starting in 2015, expanding our reach and resources.

Our history is a testament to the strength and resilience of the Rwandan people, and it continues to inspire us to push the boundaries of what is possible.

Community Impact

Through our dedicated programs, we have transformed hundreds of lives by creating local job opportunities, enhancing access to quality education, and providing critical support to people with disabilities. Our efforts have empowered individuals to break free from the cycle of poverty and achieve lasting success.

Some highlights of our community impact include:

  • Empowering over 387 students with scholarships and educational resources.
  • Facilitating international job placements for 145 individuals.
  • Creating hundreds of local job opportunities through targeted vocational training.
  • Providing essential mentorship and healthcare support to 184 people with disabilities.

Each success story reinforces our belief in the transformative power of community-driven change and the importance of investing in human potential.

Our Work

Education & Vocational Training

Our education programs provide scholarships and vocational training that empower individuals with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills. We offer modern curricula, hands-on workshops, and access to state-of-the-art facilities.

Participants gain the confidence and competence needed to pursue higher education and professional careers, transforming their lives and communities.

Job Creation & Entrepreneurship

We support aspiring entrepreneurs and job seekers by offering tailored mentorship, micro-finance opportunities, and practical business training. Our programs bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world application.

This approach not only helps individuals launch and grow their businesses but also contributes to the creation of sustainable local employment.

Support for People with Disabilities

Our initiatives for people with disabilities include access to essential services such as healthcare, tailored education, and specialized vocational training. We work diligently to create an inclusive environment where everyone can thrive.

By removing barriers and promoting adaptive learning, we help each individual achieve independence and contribute meaningfully to society.

Women’s Empowerment

Our Women’s Empowerment center focuses on leadership development, personal growth, and health through sports and wellness programs. We offer training sessions, mentorship opportunities, and networking events to foster self-confidence and independence.

This dedicated approach empowers women to take on leadership roles and become influential agents of change within their communities.

Career Guidance Center

Our Career Guidance Center provides personalized counseling, professional development workshops, and job placement services designed to prepare individuals for successful careers. We offer continuous support, from resume building to interview preparation, ensuring that every participant is job-ready.

Through partnerships with local businesses and international organizations, we create real-world opportunities that help bridge the gap between education and employment.
